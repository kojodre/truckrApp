About

Truckr is giving cargo owners such as manufacturing companies, fast moving consumer goods, distributors, exporters, importers and freight forwarders the ability to book for any kind of haulage truck to transport their goods just by a click of a button. Truckr also offers cargo owners the luxury of tracking their goods with our accurate tracking technology during transportation and can also message or call the truck drivers on the platform.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees. Receive itemized invoices to alleviate accounting burdens.

Real-time Visibility

Pinpoint the exact location of your cargo from pickup to delivery. Receive alerts to deal with issues as they arise.

Efficiency & Save Cost

Save time on booking and pay lesser than with traditional agents and brokers.

Fluid Execution

Get matched with a network of 4,000+ vetted trucks and drivers, a full insurance coverage and 24/7 guaranteed support.
